WHY I CHOSE THIS SITE

I CHOSE SILENCIO.ES BECAUSE IT'S AN AWARD-WINNING DESIGN STUDIO SITE THAT TREATS THE WEB AS BOTH PRODUCT AND MANIFESTO. IT'S A GREAT EXAMPLE OF A STUDIO PRESENTING ITSELF AS A DIGITAL PRODUCT STORE—AMBITIOUS, RISKY, AND VISUALLY CURATED.

INITIAL IMPRESSION

THE SITE READS AS AN EXPERIENCE: BOLD, CONCEPTUAL, AND SLIGHTLY THEATRICAL. IT FEELS LIKE A GALLERY/INSTALLATION MORE THAN A CONVENTIONAL STUDIO SITE. IT SPARKS CURIOSITY AND FEELS SELF-AWARE.

STRUCTURE

THE HOMEPAGE IS NARRATIVE-DRIVEN AND MINIMAL. MAIN NAVIGATION IS DELIBERATELY SPARSE (THE PORTFOLIO IS THE MAIN DESTINATION). CONTENT IS REVEALED VERTICALLY AS YOU SCROLL—DESIGNED TO BE CONSUMED LINEARLY RATHER THAN DISCOVERED VIA AN EXHAUSTIVE MENU.

VISUAL & INTERACTION

VISUAL: BLACK/WHITE/NEUTRAL WITH RESTRAINED ACCENTS. MODERN SANS SERIF, GENEROUS HIERARCHY. BRANDING STRONG AND CONSISTENT ACROSS PAGES. INTERACTION: PLENTY OF SCROLL-DRIVEN TRANSITIONS, HOVER RESPONSES, AND MICROINTERACTIONS. INTERACTIONS ARE USED AS PART OF THE STORYTELLING.

USABILITY & TECHNICAL

CLEAR BRAND VOICE AND HIERARCHY, BUT NAVIGATION CAN BE OPAQUE FOR USERS SEEKING QUICK FACTS. ACCESSIBILITY MAY BE LIMITED IF SEMANTIC MARKUP AND ARIA FALLBACKS AREN'T PRESENT. HEAVY VISUAL/INTERACTIVE SITES CAN RISK LCP AND CLS ISSUES.

CONCLUSION

STRENGTHS: POWERFUL IDENTITY AND MEMORABLE EXPERIENCE THROUGH CREATIVE DESIGN AND PURPOSEFUL CONTENT CURATION. WEAKNESSES: POTENTIAL ACCESSIBILITY AND PERFORMANCE TRADE-OFFS. LEARNING: BALANCE SPECTACLE WITH CLEAR ROUTES TO IMPORTANT INFORMATION AND ENSURE TECHNICAL OPTIMIZATIONS ARE IN PLACE.

WHY I CHOSE THIS SITE

I PICKED DONTBOARDME.COM BECAUSE IT'S A SERVICE SITE (IN-HOME PET CARE) WHERE UX NEEDS TO PRIORITIZE TRUST AND CLARITY OVER PURE AESTHETICS—USEFUL TO COMPARE AGAINST MORE EXPERIMENTAL SITES.

INITIAL IMPRESSION

WARM, FRIENDLY, AND TRUST-ORIENTED. THE COPY AND VISUALS LEAN INTO CARE AND RELIABILITY RATHER THAN FLASHY DESIGN.

STRUCTURE

CONVENTIONAL SERVICE LAYOUT: HERO WITH CTA, SERVICES, HOW IT WORKS, TESTIMONIALS, PRICING, CONTACT. THIS MATCHES USER EXPECTATIONS FOR A LOCAL SERVICE.

VISUAL & INTERACTION

VISUAL: NEUTRAL PALETTE WITH FRIENDLY ACCENTS; CLEAN SANS-SERIF TYPOGRAPHY; STRONG BRAND NAME AND IMAGERY SUPPORTIVE OF TRUST. INTERACTION: SUBTLE ENTRY/SCROLL TRANSITIONS AND HOVER STATES. MICROINTERACTIONS ARE MODEST AND PURPOSEFUL.

USABILITY & TECHNICAL

GOOD CLARITY AND CTA PLACEMENT, BUT POTENTIAL FOR IMPROVED CONVERSION WITH ALWAYS-VISIBLE BOOKING CTA AND FASTER ACCESS TO PRICING/ESTIMATES. ACCESSIBILITY CHECKS RECOMMENDED. OPTIMIZE IMAGES AND AUDIT CORE WEB VITALS.

CONCLUSION

STRENGTHS: CLARITY, TRUST, CONVERSION FLOW. WEAKNESSES: POSSIBLE PERFORMANCE/ACCESSIBILITY GAPS. LEARNING: QUICK CONVERSION ROUTES AND CAREFUL ASSET OPTIMIZATION MATTER A LOT FOR SERVICE SITES.

WELCOMETOBERK

WEB PREVIEW

WHY I CHOSE THIS SITE

I CHOSE WELCOMETOBERK.COM BECAUSE IT'S A CINEMATIC FRANCHISE SITE THAT MUST BALANCE IMMERSIVE WORLD-BUILDING WITH TICKETING AND PROMOTIONAL OBJECTIVES—AN INTERESTING UX CHALLENGE.

INITIAL IMPRESSION

CINEMATIC, IMMERSIVE, POLISHED. THE SITE INVITES EXPLORATION AND WORLD-BUILDING, WHICH IS IDEAL FOR A FILM AUDIENCE AND FANS.

STRUCTURE

INTERACTIVE ENTRY (MAP/WORLD), SECTIONS FOR VIDEOS, INFORMATION, SIGN-UP, AND TICKETS. THE STRUCTURE PRIORITIZES NARRATIVE EXPLORATION FIRST, TRANSACTIONS SECOND.

VISUAL & INTERACTION

VISUAL: RICH, CINEMATIC PALETTE; TEXTURES AND IMAGERY CONSISTENT WITH FRANCHISE BRANDING; LARGE MEDIA ASSETS AND STYLIZED TYPOGRAPHIC TREATMENTS. INTERACTION: LIKELY INTERACTIVE MAP ELEMENTS, PARALLAX/SCROLL ANIMATIONS, MEDIA OVERLAYS AND HOVER EFFECTS—AMBITIOUS INTERACTIVE LAYERS.

USABILITY & TECHNICAL

IMMERSION IS STRONG BUT MAY OBSCURE QUICK ACCESS TO TRAILERS, SHOWTIMES, OR TICKETING. ACCESSIBILITY AND MOBILE PERFORMANCE ARE TWO CORE RISKS. LARGE MEDIA AND INTERACTIVE LAYERS CAN HURT LCP, CLS AND INTERACTIVITY METRICS.

CONCLUSION

STRENGTHS: POWERFUL BRAND ALIGNMENT AND IMMERSIVE UX. WEAKNESSES: PERFORMANCE AND ACCESSIBILITY RISKS. LEARNING: PRIORITIZE DIRECT ROUTES TO KEY TASKS (TRAILERS, TICKETS) AND BAKE PERFORMANCE/ACCESSIBILITY INTO THE BUILD.
